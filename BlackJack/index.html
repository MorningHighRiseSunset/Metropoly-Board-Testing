Blackjack Instructions

Card Points

  • Ace (A): 1 or 11 points
  • 2-10: Face value
  • Jack (J), Queen (Q), King (K): 10 points

How to Play

  • Place your bet by clicking a yellow square.
  • Click Deal to receive two cards.
  • Click Hit to draw another card, or Stand to keep your hand.
  • If your hand exceeds 21, you bust and lose.
  • Dealer draws until reaching 17 or higher.
  • Closest to 21 without busting wins. Ties are a push.
