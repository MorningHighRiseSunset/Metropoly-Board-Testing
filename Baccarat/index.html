Baccarat Rules

  • Choose your bet amount and type: Player, Banker, or Tie.
  • Click Deal to start the round.
  • Both Player and Banker are dealt two cards. A third card may be drawn based on the rules.
  • The hand closest to 9 wins. 10s and face cards count as 0. If the total is over 9, only the last digit counts (e.g., 15 = 5).
  • Payouts: Player: 1:1, Banker: 0.95:1, Tie: 8:1.
  • If you win, your winnings are added to your balance. If you lose, your bet is lost.
  • Click Reset to start a new game.

Enjoy the game! For more details, see official Baccarat rules online.
